What is Positive Leadership?

The goal for this suggested topic is to share ideas /insights about positive leadership. Positive leadership starts with a focus on our own emotional intelligence and a focus on how we approach challenges and frustrations.

Self-Awareness and Self-Management

The goal for this suggested topic is to have circle members think about their emotional intelligence and specifically think about how they can increase their self-awareness and self-management. Self-awareness is the key to being able to work with others and create a positive leadership environment.

Relationship Management.

The goal for this suggested topic is to provide ideas for improving relationships and to get circle participants to think about their current relationships and how they can enhance those relationships. Aleader that is effective at managing relationships is able to: inspire others, optimize resources, seek new solutions, sustain focus, promote trust, and monitor goals and progress..

Inspirational Leadership

The goal for this suggested topic is to provide ideas for improving collaboration and then to discuss the circle members’ experiences as they begin to apply their new knowledge. The Mentor may want to draw out both good and bad experiences of the circle members, and then using the materials provided or added by the Mentor, discuss ways collaboration could have been improved. Toward the end of the engagement it would expected that circle members begin to share their results.
